exporting/publishing your video

how you export your video can change based on the platform you're planning to publish it on.

for example, i've been recording in vertical format when i know i'm making a video for instagram. this way, it shows up on the feed taking up more real estate on the screen. it also offers a more seamless viewing experience when you can just watch the vieo in the feed and then expand it to fill up the screen without having to rotate your phone. most of the time, you're recording and exporting in horizontal format to fit desktop and tv screens.

here's a kicker - not all platforms are created equal when publishing a video. some are more friendly than others. youtube and vimeo are the most friendly as in they won't compress your video and instead will display it in it's full quality. instagram and facebook on the other hand aren't as nice. there is nothing more frustrating than making a high quality video and then watching instagram's compression absolutely kill it. something worth mentioning related to this - IGTV is the way to go for videos. if your video is over a minute long, you can release it on instagram as an IGTV and more of the video quality is retained.
